# Product Requirements Document - Connect Raitu

## 1. Startup Vision & Positioning

**Core Problem Statement:**
Farmers in India suffer from fragmented supply chains, lack of access to quality inputs, poor technical guidance, and opaque market pricing, leading to low yields and reduced profitability.

**Value Proposition:**
Connect Raitu provides a unified, full-stack digital platform that empowers farmers with direct access to quality inputs, AI-driven crop advisory, and transparent market linkages, while streamlining the supply chain for retailers.

**Differentiation (Year 1):**
- **AI-First Approach:** Integrated AI Plant Doctor and Crop Planner from Day 1, not as an afterthought.
- **Hyper-Local Focus:** Deep localization in language and crop advisory specific to pilot districts.
- **Unified Ecosystem:** Seamless integration of Farmer, Retailer, and Admin workflows.

## 2. Complete Feature List

### A. Farmer Mobile App (Android-first)
- **Input Marketplace:** Seeds, fertilizers, pesticides, tools with search and filtering.
- **AI Plant Doctor:** Image upload, diagnosis, product recommendation.
- **AI Crop Planner:** Soil/weather/crop/irrigation modeling.
- **Localized Support:** Multi-language interface.
- **Commerce:** Cart, checkout, COD, UPI, Wallet, Real-time tracking.
- **Advisory:** Agri advisories, Weather dashboard, Mandi prices.
- **Engagement:** Subscription packs, Loyalty rewards, Referrals.
- **Profile:** Farmer profile, Farm mapping.
- **Support:** Chat with agronomist (AI + Human).

### B. Retailer/Dealer App
- **Procurement:** Wholesale pricing, Bulk ordering.
- **Inventory:** Management and tracking.
- **Finance:** Credit options, Payment history.
- **Logistics:** Delivery scheduling.

### C. Logistics & Internal Ops
- **Delivery App:** Agent interface, Route optimization.
- **Warehouse:** WMS, Stock management.
- **Fleet:** Assignment and tracking.

### D. Admin Dashboard
- **Operations:** Order management, Farmer KYC.
- **Analytics:** Insights, Marketing automation.
- **Catalog:** Product addition/update.
- **Growth:** Coupon & referral management.

### E. Payment Integration
- **Methods:** UPI, Razorpay/Stripe, Wallet.
- **Features:** Credit limits (retailers), GST invoicing, Refund workflows.

## 8. Unit Economics & Business Model

- **CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost):** Blended cost of offline activation + digital marketing.
- **LTV (Lifetime Value):** Projected 3-year value based on seasonal input cycles.
- **AOV (Average Order Value):** Target AOV for inputs vs. tools.
- **Contribution Margin:** Target margin after logistics and COGS.
- **Pricing Strategy:** Competitive SKU pricing to undercut incumbents via efficient supply chain.

## 9. GTM Strategy (First 12 Months)

- **Pilot:** Launch in 3 selected districts.
- **Acquisition:** Village-level entrepreneurs (VLEs) for farmer onboarding.
- **Retailers:** Direct onboarding of key influencers/dealers.
- **Marketing:** Combined offline (mela activations) and online (WhatsApp, Facebook).
- **Cycles:** Seasonal push aligned with Kharif and Rabi seasons.

## 10. Risk Assessment

- **Operational:** Supply chain disruptions, warehouse mismanagement.
- **Credit:** Retailer default risks (mitigated by credit limits).
- **Logistics:** Last-mile delivery failures in remote areas.
- **External:** Weather volatility affecting crop cycles and demand.
- **Tech:** App adoption barriers, connectivity issues.
- **Legal:** Compliance with agri-input regulations and data privacy.
