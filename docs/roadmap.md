# Implementation Roadmap - Connect Raitu (6 Months)

## Phase 1: Foundation & MVP (Months 1-2)

### Sprint 1-2: Setup & Auth
- **Tech:** Setup Monorepo (Next.js + React Native), CI/CD pipelines.
- **Backend:** Auth Service (OTP), User Profiles (Farmer/Retailer).
- **Frontend:** Onboarding screens, Login flow.

### Sprint 3-4: Core Marketplace
- **Backend:** Product Catalog Service, Inventory Service.
- **Frontend:** Home Dashboard, Product Listing, Search.
- **Design:** Finalize Design System & Component Library.

## Phase 2: Commerce & Logistics (Months 3-4)

### Sprint 5-6: Cart & Checkout
- **Backend:** Order Service, Payment Gateway Integration (Razorpay/UPI).
- **Frontend:** Cart management, Checkout flow, Order success.

### Sprint 7-8: Retailer & Admin
- **Retailer App:** Wholesale catalog, Order placement.
- **Admin Panel:** Product management, Order tracking dashboard.

## Phase 3: AI & Growth (Months 5-6)

### Sprint 9-10: AI Features
- **AI Plant Doctor:** Integrate ML model, Image upload flow.
- **AI Crop Planner:** Rule engine + LLM integration.

### Sprint 11-12: Polish & Launch
- **QA:** End-to-end testing, Bug fixes.
- **Pilot:** Launch in 3 districts.
- **Marketing:** Onboard first 100 retailers and 1000 farmers.

## Team Requirements
- **Engineering:** 2 Full-stack, 2 Mobile (React Native), 1 AI/ML Engineer.
- **Design:** 1 Product Designer.
- **Product:** 1 PM/Agronomist.
- **Ops:** 1 Logistics Manager.
